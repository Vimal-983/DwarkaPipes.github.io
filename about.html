For many decades, DP Pipes company is rated as one of the most renowned distribution of plastic pipe systems in India. With many years of dealing with pvc pipe products, today DP Pipes has emerged as the most reliable name in the plastic pipe . During 1990, DP pipes further expanded to dealing and marketing Braided PVC Hoses and Flat Delivery Hoses as well as Rubber Sheets which was again first time in India as convenient and economical alternative to rubber hoses. Today, with four decades of dealing in plastics processing, DP Pipes has emerged as a leading supplier of plastic pipes and fittings in India.
